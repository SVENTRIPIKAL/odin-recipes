Easy Homemade Pizza Dough

Photo by Christian Holzinger on Unsplash

Description

An easy and beginner-friendly recipe for making soft pizza dough
with only just a few everyday ingredients that will make you forget
why you ever considered frozen store-bought pizza crusts.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
  • 1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 2 1/2 cups bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
